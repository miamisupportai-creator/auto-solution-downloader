The Problem We're Solving

With 222K Instagram followers and 24/7 operations across Instagram, Facebook, and WhatsApp, 1-800-NO-FAULT is receiving an estimated 80–150+ inbound messages per day across platforms. Each message requires a human to read it, determine if it's a qualified lead, ask follow-up questions (accident type, location, injury severity), and route it to the right Advisor. That's a minimum of 5–8 minutes per message — adding up to 10–20 hours of manual work every single day for your social team.

⚠Slow response time kills conversions. Accident victims contact multiple services simultaneously. The first to respond wins. Manual triaging means hours of delay — your competitors respond faster.
⚠Same 5 intake questions, 150 times a day. "What type of accident?", "Where did it happen?", "Are you injured?" — this is repetitive, automatable work that drains your team's capacity.
⚠Content creation doesn't scale to 8 cities. Your Instagram presence is strong (756 posts, active reels) but producing consistent, localized content for Miami, Houston, NYC, Atlanta manually is a bottleneck.
⚠After-hours gaps despite "24/7" branding. Your brand promises around-the-clock service — but human-dependent intake means late-night and weekend messages sit unanswered until morning.
